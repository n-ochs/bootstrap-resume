ABOUT ME

Nick Ochs graduated from St. John's University in 2018 with a Bachelor's of Science degree in Quantitative Risk Management & Insurance. Directly after, he was hired by Navigators Insurance Company as an underwriting trainee in their commercial auto division. While quickly learning the ropes of the insurance industry, Nick was able to facilitate relationships with wholesale brokers and start builing a profitable book of business. He was soon promoted to an Underwriter, assuming new responsibilities and challenges.
